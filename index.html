CDC Biohazard Alert

2018 Ann Arbor, Michigan USA

On December 1, 2018 the WHO declared the downtown Ann Arbor, Michigan area to be at extreme risk for spread of two infectous diseases: Carbapenem-resistant Enterobacteriaceae (CrE1) and Methicillin-resistant Staphylococcus Aurus (M-rSA5a). The Ann Arbor office of the CDC-VC is currently monitoring the situation and has multiple teams in the area investigating.
